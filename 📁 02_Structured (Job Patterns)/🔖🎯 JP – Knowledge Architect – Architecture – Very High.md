STRUCTURE

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Design and govern knowledge architecture: taxonomy, structure, governance in Zendesk
- Manage full content lifecycle — gap identification, commissioning SMEs, retiring stale content
- Structure content for AI/ML readability to power ticket deflection, chatbots, agent-assist
- Champion knowledge culture across Support, Product, and Engineering
- Define and track KPIs: search success rate, deflection rate, content gaps
- Establish cross-functional sync channels to capture knowledge before it hits support

REQUIREMENTS:
- 4+ years in Knowledge Management, Content Strategy, Technical Writing, or Senior Support Ops
- Proven experience designing KM systems (Zendesk Guide essential)
- Demonstrated understanding of how structured content fuels AI/ML (chatbots, search indexing)
- Strong information architecture and editorial skills
- Analytical skills using Zendesk/Jira data to measure ROI and find gaps
- Ability to influence without authority

NICE TO HAVE:
- Cross-functional collaboration (Sales, Success, Product, Engineering)
- CMS experience beyond ticketing platforms
- Familiarity with SQL / data pipelines
- Experience supporting multiple enterprise SaaS products
- KCS Certification, Zendesk Admin Certification, PMP
- Master's in Information Architecture or Knowledge Management
- Restaurant Technology experience

FUNCTION (OKA INTENT)

DO:
Build and govern the knowledge infrastructure that connects structured content to AI systems,
support agents, and customer self-service — across multiple complex SaaS products.

FIX:
Knowledge is fragmented, unstructured, and not machine-readable. AI deflection is underperforming.
Support agents lack a reliable single source of truth. New product knowledge isn't captured
before it surfaces as support tickets.

CHECK:
- AI ticket deflection rate
- Search success rate
- Content gap closure rate
- Time-to-documentation for new product releases
- Support agent usage and self-service resolution rate

CONSTRAINT:
- Must work within Zendesk Guide ecosystem
- Influence without authority — no direct ownership over SMEs or product teams
- Must serve both human readability AND machine readability simultaneously
- Multi-product environment (Crunchtime + Zenput) increases complexity

INTERPRETATION

CORE IDEA:
Crunchtime is not hiring a writer. They are hiring someone to architect the knowledge layer
that their AI systems run on — and to own that layer end-to-end.

IMPLIED NEED:
Their AI deflection tools are underperforming because the underlying knowledge isn't structured
for machine consumption. This role exists to fix the foundation, not produce more content.

MATURITY LEVEL: Architecture
