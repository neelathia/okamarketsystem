> Financial services companies aren't hiring documentation leads. They are hiring governors of institutional memory.

**In Regulated Industries, Documentation Is Compliance Infrastructure**

The Technical Writing Lead role at a major financial institution lists AI proficiency as a core requirement — alongside governance, regulatory compliance, and quality review processes.

That combination is not accidental.

In regulated industries, documentation is not knowledge management. It is audit readiness. Every process that isn't documented is a liability. Every document that is inconsistent is a risk. Every AI-generated output that isn't validated is an exposure.

The institution is not looking for a better writer or a faster documentation team. It is looking for someone who can govern AI-assisted documentation at scale without introducing compliance risk.

This is documentation as risk infrastructure — and the market hasn't named it that yet.
