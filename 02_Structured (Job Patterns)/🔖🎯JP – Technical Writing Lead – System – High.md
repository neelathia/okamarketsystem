STRUCTURE

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Lead, mentor, and develop a team of technical writers
- Balance team workload and align with project milestones and timelines
- Collaborate across multiple teams to align documentation needs and outcomes
- Communicate with stakeholders at multiple levels
- Establish and reinforce quality review and accuracy standards
- Integrate governance requirements for documentation sustainability and compliance
- Oversee documentation strategies for onboarding, training, and upskilling

REQUIREMENTS:
- Proven leadership in managing and mentoring technical writing teams
- Strong organizational skills for milestone-driven delivery
- Advanced cross-team collaboration and communication skills
- Experience establishing governance and quality review for documentation
- AI proficiency: prompting, iterative refinement, output validation

NICE TO HAVE:
- Enterprise onboarding and training strategy familiarity
- Knowledge of regulatory requirements in IT environments
- Confluence, SharePoint, advanced documentation platforms
- Learning design and knowledge transfer methodologies

---

FUNCTION (OKA INTENT)

DO:
Lead a team of technical writers in a large financial institution to produce governed, high-quality documentation that supports onboarding, training, and organizational knowledge transfer.

FIX:
Documentation quality, consistency, and governance are insufficient to support the scale and compliance requirements of a large regulated institution.

CHECK:
Milestone delivery, documentation quality standards, team performance, onboarding effectiveness, governance compliance.

CONSTRAINT:
Regulated financial services environment. Compliance and governance requirements shape what can be documented and how.

---

INTERPRETATION

CORE IDEA:
In regulated industries, documentation is not just knowledge infrastructure — it is audit infrastructure. This role governs both.

IMPLIED NEED:
A large institution needs someone who can enforce documentation discipline at scale without losing velocity. The AI proficiency requirement signals that they also want someone who can modernize the function, not just manage it.

MATURITY LEVEL:
System

RELATED ROLES:
- AI & Automation Knowledge Implementation Manager (governance orientation)
- Principal Technical Writer (standards and quality bar)

PATTERN:
Large regulated institutions are adding AI proficiency requirements to documentation leadership roles — not because they want AI output, but because they need leaders who can govern AI-assisted documentation processes.

---

YOUR POSITION:
Regulated environments treat documentation as compliance infrastructure. The person who leads this function isn't managing writers — they are managing institutional memory at the intersection of knowledge, risk, and AI adoption. That is a different job than most "Technical Writing Lead" titles suggest.
